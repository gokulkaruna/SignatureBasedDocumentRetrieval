Title: OnePlus Nord Buds 2 review: Good sound, noise cancelling feature and more
The OnePlus Nord Buds 2 is the latest addition to OnePlus' audio product lineup, promising to deliver high-quality
sound, noise cancelling features, and seamless connectivity. The earbuds are priced at Rs 2999 and come with a
compact design and advanced features. Here is a two-page summary of the review of the OnePlus Nord Buds 2:
Design and Build:
The OnePlus Nord Buds 2 have a sleek and modern design that's both stylish and functional. The earbuds are small
and lightweight, making them comfortable to wear for extended periods. The charging case is also compact and easy
to carry around in your pocket or bag. The earbuds come with three different sizes of silicone ear tips, allowing you
to find the perfect fit for your ears.
Connectivity:
The OnePlus Nord Buds 2 uses Bluetooth 5.2 for seamless connectivity with your smartphone, laptop, or tablet. The
earbuds automatically connect to your device when you take them out of the charging case, and you can control
playback and volume using touch controls on the earbuds. The earbuds also support fast pairing with OnePlus
devices, making it easy to switch between different devices.
Sound Quality:
The OnePlus Nord Buds 2 delivers good sound quality, with clear and balanced audio across different genres of
music. The earbuds come with 11mm dynamic drivers that produce rich bass and clear treble, making them ideal for
listening to music, podcasts, or audiobooks. The earbuds also come with noise cancelling features that help block out
external noise and improve the overall listening experience.
Battery Life:
The OnePlus Nord Buds 2 has a battery life of up to 7 hours on a single charge, and the charging case provides an
additional 23 hours of battery life. The earbuds also support fast charging, with 10 minutes of charging providing up
to 10 hours of playback time.
